Our Origins

Stella Park International Academy (SPIA) is a visionary initiative launched by IPSDI-Burundi, an organization known for its commitment to sustainable development and community empowerment. SPIA is a symbol of academic transformation in Burundi, offering a model of inclusive and high-quality education that nurtures the full potential of every learner.

Our School & Where We Are

Nestled on the slopes of Kamakara hill in Cibitoke Province, SPIA offers a peaceful and conducive learning environment. With a strategic location near Rwanda and the DRC, the school is accessible to students from across Burundi and East Africa. Our modern facilities are set on a campus with views of the Rugeregere Valley.

What Does “Excellence” Mean to Us?

At SPIA, excellence means putting the learner at the center. We offer personalized and collective learning that nurtures each student’s talents, skills, and values, fostering success through empowerment—not elitism.

Learning at the Center of Our Activities

With small class sizes and personalized coaching, we believe every child can succeed. SPIA promotes active learning, emotional well-being, and character formation through a learner-centered approach.

Girls' Empowerment & Gender Equality

SPIA is committed to gender equality and inclusive education:

  • Equal role distribution for boys and girls
  • Zero tolerance for gender-based violence and discrimination
  • Fair leadership opportunities and awareness campaigns
  • Health and wellness education for all

Student Coaching & Mentorship

Our coaching program helps students become confident, organized, and resilient. Through follow-up tools and regular mentorship, we support academic, personal, and spiritual development.
