Middle School Academic Curriculum (Grades 7–9)

Aligned with U.S. Standards | Compliant with MSA Accreditation Requirements

Stella Park International Academy provides a rigorous, balanced, and Christ-centered curriculum for Grades 7 through 9, in full alignment with U.S. educational standards and Middle States Association (MSA) accreditation expectations. The curriculum promotes academic excellence, character formation, critical thinking, and global citizenship through integrated disciplines and student-centered pedagogy.

  • Weekly hours capped at 40 per grade
  • Total GPA-equivalent yearly credits = 8.0 per grade
  • Inclusion of local languages (Kirundi and French)
  • Emphasis on Leadership, Faith, and Entrepreneurial Skills
  • Strong foundation in core academic subjects

Observations & Narrative Justification
  • Balanced Curriculum: Each grade maintains a total of 40 instructional hours per week and 8.0 credits per year, supporting a GPA scale of 4.0.
  • Language Development: English is intensified to prepare students for academic fluency, while French and Kirundi ensure bilingual/multilingual competence and cultural continuity.
  • Faith Integration: Bible Studies and Christian leadership are woven into the curriculum to promote spiritual growth and moral integrity.
  • STEM Readiness: Emphasis on Mathematics, Integrated Science, and IT builds a strong foundation for future STEM-related pathways.
  • Global & Local Relevance: Courses like Entrepreneurship, Business Education, and Leadership empower learners with 21st-century skills, while including local history and languages grounds them in their community.
