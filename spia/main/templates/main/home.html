About Stella Park International Academy

Located in Kamakara, Cibitoke Province, SPIA is dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. We provide a world-class curriculum that prepares students for global opportunities.

Academic Excellence

We offer a rigorous curriculum designed to develop critical thinking and innovation.

Global Perspective

Our programs prepare students for international opportunities, including IELTS & TOEFL certification.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we focus on leadership, sports, and extracurricular excellence.

Boarding

We offer state-of-the-art boarding facilities that ensure students have a comfortable and secure environment to thrive in their education.

Location

SPIA is located in Kamakara, Cibitoke Province, offering a peaceful and conducive learning environment.
