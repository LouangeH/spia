Welcome From Head of School

Our vision is to develop lifelong learners who can promote peace in today’s fast-changing world. We aim to fully equip them with holistic skills that will prepare them to diligently serve and offer reflective solutions to 21st-century challenges, including globalization, technological advancements, and shifting demographics.

SPIA welcomes students aged **1.5 to 19 years**, offering both **Day and Boarding** options. Our facilities meet the highest **international education standards**, including **ICT and Science laboratories** tailored for Cambridge and IB curricula.

Nestled along the shores of **Lake Tanganyika**, SPIA provides a **serene learning environment**. Our structured social, moral, and academic discipline fosters a holistic approach to education. The mentorship program led by the Welfare Office ensures student development beyond academics.

Mme Goreth – Head of School

Our History

Inspired by leading international schools in the region, the founders aimed to provide access to Internationally renowned education programs for Burundian and International students in Burundi and cultivate strong global citizenship. In September 2018, Cubahiro International School became the first in Burundi to implement the Cambridge Advanced International Certificate of Education (AICE) Curriculum. The school’s set high standards led to its inauguration by H.E Pierre Nkurunziza, the President of Burundi, on October 15, 2018. In 2023, Cubahiro International School became the first school in Burundi to express interest to offer the prestigious International Baccalaureate (IB) program. CIS is now a candidate school for both IBMYP and IBDP. The founders’ mission is to foster excellence and community service as it is embodied in the school’s motto: ” Striving for excellence and Humility in Service. “
