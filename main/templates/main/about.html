Our Story

Stella Park International Academy is not just a school—it's a community committed to excellence, not elitism. Our belief is that true excellence involves lifting each other up and offering personalized education that adapts to each student's needs and potential. SPIA nurtures talents, fosters growth, and promotes fairness for every learner, regardless of their background.

What Do We Mean by “Excellence”?

Excellence at SPIA means placing the learner at the center of all our actions. We promote individual growth, nurture unique talents, and adapt learning to each student's pace and needs. It’s about collaboration, creativity, and respect—not competition or elitism.

Our Origins & Our Promoter

Stella Park International Academy (SPIA) is a visionary initiative launched by IPSDI-Burundi, an organization known for its commitment to sustainable development and community empowerment. SPIA is a symbol of academic transformation in Burundi, offering a model of inclusive and high-quality education that nurtures the full potential of every learner.

Our School & Where We Are

Nestled on the slopes of Kamakara hill in Cibitoke Province, SPIA offers a peaceful and conducive learning environment. With a strategic location near Rwanda and the DRC, the school is accessible to students from across Burundi and East Africa. Our modern facilities are set on a 3-hectare campus with views of the Rugeregere Valley.
